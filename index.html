Food Trip

Egg and Bacon Canape

These small sandwiches with cream cheese, bacon, and quail egg look beautiful on the holiday table and serve as a great snack.

Ham and Cheese Stacks

The savory stack features slices of tender smoky ham and luscious Brie cheese. The sweet stack is slathered in marmalade and topped with a dollop of whipped cream.

Fried Prosciutto Tortellini

Fried Prosciutto Tortellini is the perfect party appetizer recipe. Bites of crispy, crunchy pasta filled with cheese and even better when dipped in classic marinara.

Braised Chicken Legs With Grapes and Fennel

Sweet fennel and honey, spicy Calabrian chile paste, and red wine vinegar for brightness. Red or green table grapes are sweet but have just the right amount of acidity for this dish.

Double-Stack Mushroom and Chicken Cheesburger

These Chicken Burgers are grilled and finished with gooey Muenster, stacked, and topped with shredded Lettuce, Sweet Pickles, and Curry Mustard.

Caesar Salad Roast Chicken

Chicken smothered in Caesar dressing and roasted until the garlic, anchovies, and mustard become deeply caramelized and flavorful.

Tiramisu

An Italian original recipe. It is made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa

Halo-halo
